About

I currently live in Seattle, Washington with my wife and two dogs. Outside of work I am an avid mountain biker, and enjoy spending as much time as possible in the outdoors. I am also passionate about music and music technology -- I enjoy playing music (primarily guitar), dabbling with home recording, building guitar amplifiers, and from 2014 to 2020 I was co-instructor for the Music Engineering Lab at the University of Pittsburgh. I also maintain and manage the web presence for Volcano Guitar Works, a small musical instrument supply company founded in 1983 by my parents.
